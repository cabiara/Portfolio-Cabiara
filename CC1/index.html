University of the Cordilleras

The University of the Cordilleras is a private university in Baguio City, Philippines. The school is in the heart of the city, near Our Lady of Atonement Church (the Baguio Cathedral), hotels, restaurants, and shopping malls. It has Autonomous status and is an ISO certified university, formerly known as Baguio Colleges Foundation (BCF).

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program provides the students with an in-depth understanding on the theoretical and applied aspects of computing. Students gain knowledge of the theories along with the methodologies and techniques in the research and development of multifaceted software systems as well as new knowledge in the field of computing.
